Hydrogen Line Telescope
Since I started experimentation with SDR recievers in early 2020, I've always wanted to build my own radio telescope for things like pulsar detection and recieiving data from SpaceX Falcon 9 second stages, and I've finally started taking steps in that direction by doing something a little simpler - detecting the microwave radiation emitted by neutral hydrogen atoms undergoing a spin-flip transition in our milky way.
